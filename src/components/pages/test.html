    Are you organizing a photo exposition or do you need a larger order? Contact us know what you are looking for. Please add details for sizes and amounts, so we can give you a correct quote.
  • Delivery & returns
    Your Maralex order will be ready to ship within 3 working days. We work with a 100% customer satisfaction guarantee, meaning we will solve any issues that may occur, together with our customer. Therefore, we don't have a standardized returns policy.
  • Shipping
    Shipping is free for orders with a total value of CHF 75 or more. Else, the shipping costs are €5,99 in The Netherlands and €9,99 for the rest of Europe. Shipping within the US and Canada: $9.99.
  • How is quality assured?
    We'll help you decide on the right size for your Maralex print during the ordering process. When you've uploaded your photo, our system lets you know if your photo doesn't have the required resolution for the chosen Maralex size. That's how we ensure the best possible results.
  • File/Image requirements
    Minimum resolution: 80ppi Color space: sRGB
